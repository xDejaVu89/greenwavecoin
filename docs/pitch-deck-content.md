## Slide 3: GreenWaveCoin Turns Idle PCs Into a Distributed AI Supercomputer

**Headline:** A permissionless DePIN network that pays workers in GWC tokens for contributing CPU to AI research

**How it works:**
1. Worker downloads the GreenWaveCoin app (Windows, 11MB)
2. App connects to the coordinator and receives NAS tasks
3. Worker trains and evaluates micro neural architectures locally
4. Results are submitted, verified, and scored
5. Epoch rewards are distributed on-chain via Merkle proof claims

**Key differentiators:**
- No GPU required — standard consumer CPUs handle the workload
- Fully on-chain reward distribution via RewardEscrowV2 smart contract on Polygon
- Open-source coordinator and worker — fully auditable
- Workers earn GWC proportional to tasks completed and accuracy scores
